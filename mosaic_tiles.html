The finished surface of every Spa is down to individual client preferences, from standard ceramic tiles to the explosive colours and styles offered by designer tiles from the likes of SICIS, Mosaico and Craig Bragdy. Tiles are adhered to the surface of our spas with market leading adhesives and epoxy grouts, designed specifically to cope with heat, flex, water and chemical resistance.

We are able to add your chosen tile design to our CAD renders so that you can see what the finished design will look like both on its own and in situ.
