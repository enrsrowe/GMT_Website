Our Products

Each and every one of our spas is sculpted from the very best raw materials on the market today, with water, chemical and heat resistant fibreglass and gelcoat materials, as used in the marine industry, and then finally coated with a heat retaining polyurethane foam.

The finished surface of every Spa is down to individual client preferences, from standard ceramic tiles to the explosive colours and styles offered by designer tiles from the likes of SICIS, Mosaico and Craig Bragdy. Tiles are adhered to the surface of our spas with market leading adhesives and epoxy grouts, designed specifically to cope with heat, flex, water and chemical resistance.

We use our in-house CAD software to produce drawings and 3D renders of our spas so that you can see what the finished design will look like both on its own and in situ.

Standard Tank

A balance tank is a much needed addition to any spa or pool. When a person gets into the pool/spa their body displaces water. The balance tank stores this water until the person gets out and the water is pumped back into the pool. Our standard tank is one piece tank with lid and access hatch. All built to client specified dimensions and volume.

Made to order.

Sectional Tank

The sectional balance tank is suitable for where installation access is an issue. The tank is made in several panels which when assembled form the complete tank. Panels are made to the clients specification in order to be fitted where space may be an issue. Our team will assemble the tank on site for you.

Made to order.

Computer Aided Design

From our 3D models we can offer our customers a pre-constructions visualisation of the finished product as it would look in situ. This can either take the form of static renders or fly through videos. Working hand in hand with the project designer, the product can easily be modified prior to any drawings being issued. The customer has pre-agreed the design at model stage so any drawings can be quickly issued for customer approval.

Modeling pipework and equipment in tight spaces can easily highlight any problems with the project before any factory or site work begins which dramatically cuts installation costs.

Delivery

We can arrange delivery for any spa direct to site. Our spas come ready to install with all jets and plumbing fitted. Large Spas for overseas clients can be palleted up for transportation.

Installation

We offer on site installation for all of our split spas and sectional balance tanks. Whether a new build project or simply replacement of an old existing spa the solution is to hand. Sited and rejoined with the same integrity as a one piece product, by our fabrication team. If it is feasible to design and fabricate we will offer a solution.
